Cancer Exercise Programs

Our programs are designed with input from oncology professionals to provide safe and effective exercises. Remember to consult your doctor before beginning.

Important Safety Reminder: You MUST consult your doctor or oncologist before starting any exercise program listed here to ensure it is appropriate for your specific condition, treatment plan, and overall health.

Gentle Mobility Routine (During Treatment)

Designed for individuals currently undergoing active treatment (chemotherapy, radiation) to help maintain joint mobility, reduce stiffness, and manage fatigue gently.

Focus: Flexibility, Light Movement Intensity: Very Light Equipment: None / Chair
View Program Details

Post-Treatment Strength Foundation

A beginner-level program focused on rebuilding muscle strength and endurance after primary cancer treatments are completed. Focuses on major muscle groups with modifications available.

Focus: Strength, Endurance Intensity: Light to Moderate Equipment: Light Weights / Resistance Bands (Optional)
View Program Details

Balance and Stability Essentials

Exercises specifically chosen to improve balance and reduce the risk of falls, which can be a concern during or after treatment due to fatigue or neuropathy.

Focus: Balance, Coordination Intensity: Light Equipment: Chair (for support)
